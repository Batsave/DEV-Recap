Welcome to **DEV Recap**, a wiki dedicated to developers.  
Here, you'll find a condensed overview of essential concepts to understand, learn, and apply the principles of designing useful, usable, and desirable digital experiences.

DEV Recap is designed as a clear and concise resource: a practical tool for review, teaching, or inspiration.

## Site Objectives

- **Provide a clear and structured summary** of development fundamentals.  
- **Offer concrete methods and tools** for students, designers, and developers.  
- **Serve as a quick reference** before an exam, project, or design workshop.
